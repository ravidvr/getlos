ℹ About getlos

getlos is a Berlin cinema map that shows every movie screening across the city — all languages, all cinemas, updated daily.

Data from berlin.de/kino (58 cinemas), English Cinema Berlin (40 cinemas), and OpenStreetMap.

Built with Leaflet and vanilla JS. Open source on GitHub. No tracking, no cookies.
